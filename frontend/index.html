Our Story

Six Years of Culinary Excellence

For over six years, Tatva has been Hyderabad's destination for those who believe vegetarian cuisine deserves the same reverence as any world-class dining experience. Our name — derived from the Sanskrit word for "essence" — reflects our philosophy: to distill the purest flavours from global kitchens and present them with artistry and warmth.

Nestled in the heart of Begumpet, Tatva brings together international culinary traditions — from Mediterranean mezze to Japanese-inspired small plates — reimagined through a purely vegetarian lens. Every dish is a celebration of ingredients, technique, and the simple joy of a beautifully shared meal.
